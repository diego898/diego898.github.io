I am excited to announce that I will be accepting an [Academic Pathways Postdoctoral Fellowship](https://gradschool.vanderbilt.edu/postdoctoral/academic-pathways/) at [Vanderbilt University](http://www.vanderbilt.edu/) in September! I will be jointly hosted in the [Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science](https://engineering.vanderbilt.edu/eecs/) in the lab of [Dr. Eugene Vorobeychik](http://vorobeychik.com/) and in the [Department of Biomedical Informatics](https://medschool.vanderbilt.edu/dbmi/) in the lab of [Dr. Thomas Lasko](https://medschool.vanderbilt.edu/dbmi/person/thomas-lasko-md-phd).
